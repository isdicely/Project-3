Measurement Score Explanation Source
Happiness Sum of the following measurements: Dystopia Residual, GDP per capita, Social Support, Healthy life expectancy, Freedom to make life choices, Generosity, Perception of corruption Link
GDP per capita Per capita gross domestic product is a metric that breaks down a country's economic output per person and is calculated by dividing the GDP of a country by its population. “Household income values in local currency are divided by the ICP-iceh PPP ratio to obtain ID. For those countries not covered by the World Bank ICP, GDP-based PPPs from the CIA World Factbook are used.” Link Link
Healthy Life Expectancy Healthy Life Expectancy is calculated by comparing the difference in years of life expectancy a certain country exceeds that of the country with the lowest life expectancy. This number is then multiplied by the estimated coefficient for life expectancy located in the Statistical Appendix. Link
Social Support National Institutions Index. Personal Economy Index. Citizen Engagement Index. Community Basics Index Link
Freedom to Make Life Choices Freedom to make life choices is the national average of responses to the GWP question “Are you satisfied or dissatisfied with your freedom to choose what you do with your life?” Link
Generosity Citizen Engagement Index. “A record’s final index score is the mean of valid items multiplied by 100. The final country-level index score is the mean of all individual records for which an index score was calculated.” Link
Corruption Perception Corruption Index. “A record’s final index score is the mean of valid items multiplied by 100. The final country-level index score is the mean of all individual records for which an index score was calculated” National Institutions index. Link
Dystopian Residual Lowest score on the Cantrill Ladder in comparison to other countries Link
Residual Error Margin of error is calculated around a proportion at the 95% confidence level. The maximum margin of error was calculated assuming a reported percentage of 50% and takes into account the design effect. Link
