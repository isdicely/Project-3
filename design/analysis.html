World Happiness Score Component Interpretation



Regression plots were used to look at the association of each component of the score with the overall value. To interpret the data the r-value are compared to cut-offs generaly used in social studies data interpretation.


    Interpretation using the r-square values for Social Sciences

  • High association: r-squared greater than 0.67
  • Moderate association: r-squared between 0.33 and 0.67
  • Low association: r-squared between 0.19 and 0.33
  • No association: r-squared below 0.19
